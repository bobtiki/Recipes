Cherry Nut Muffins

Sides, Solstice

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Servings: 12 Source: Linda Boszko

Ingredients

1¾ cups (211g) sifted all-purpose flour

¼ cup (50.5g) sugar

2½ teaspoons baking powder

¾ teaspoon salt

1 egg, well-beaten (nice & frothy)

3 ounces evaporated milk

3 ounces maraschino cherry “juice” (syrup from bottle)

⅓ cup salad oil (or melted shortening)

½ cup – 1 cup (~90g) walnuts, chopped (to taste)

6 ounce bottle of maraschino cherries, cut into quarters

Directions

Combine dry ingredients. Combine wet ingredients separately. Then, add wet to dry and combine via the muffin method (stir only until dry ingredients are moist).

Grease muffin pans or use papers (I prefer papers).

NOTE: Ideally, use a disher to scoop an even amount of batter into each muffin tin. I need to figure out the amount.

Bake at 400º for 25 minutes.

Notes

Mom used to make these every Xmas morning when I was a kid. Adapted from “Best Ever Muffins” in a previous edition of the Better Homes & Gardens Cookbook.

The three ounces each of evaporated milk + cherry juice substitute for ¾ cup milk in the original recipe, but this gives the muffins a festive pink hue and additional flavor.
